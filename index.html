    OUR COFFEE STORY

    Coffee’s journey began in Ethiopia, where a goat herder discovered its energy boost. It spread through Arabia, then Europe, making cafés cultural hubs. Today, coffee is more than a drink—it’s a ritual that unites people.

    GLOBAL VARIETIES

    Arabica offers smooth, fruity notes, while Robusta packs bold intensity. Ethiopian coffee is citrusy, Colombian is chocolatey, and Sumatran is deep and spicy. Each cup carries the essence of its origin.

    BREWING ARTS

    Brewing is both science and art. Espresso is rich, cold brew smooth, and the French press deep. Pour-over reveals delicate notes, while Turkish coffee is bold. The right grind and method unlock the perfect flavor.
